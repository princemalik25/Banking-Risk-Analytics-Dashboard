# 📊 Banking Risk Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project develops a **risk analytics dashboard for banking and financial services**, focusing on how data can be used to **minimise the risk of losing money while lending to customers**.  
The dashboard integrates demographic, financial, and relationship data to provide insights into **loan exposure, deposit behaviour, and profitability drivers**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
> *Develop a basic understanding of risk analytics in banking and financial services and understand how data is used to minimise the risk of losing money while lending to customers.*

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** → Dashboard design, interactive visuals, DAX measures  
- **Python (pandas, seaborn, numpy, matplotlib)** → Data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory analysis 
- **Excel** → Initial dataset preparation and validation  

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes **3,000 clients** with attributes such as:
- Demographics: Age, Gender, Nationality, Occupation  
- Financials: Estimated Income, Bank Loans, Deposits, Credit Card Balance, Superannuation Savings  
- Relationships: Banking Relationship (Retail, Institutional, Private Bank, Commercial), Investment Advisor (IAId)  
- Risk Indicators: Risk Weighting, Fee Structure (High, Mid, Low), Loyalty Classification  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure
- **Home / Summary**
  - Total Clients, Total Loan, Total Deposit, Total Fees  
  - High‑level KPIs for portfolio monitoring  

- **Loan Analysis**
  - Loan exposure by Income Band, Occupation, Loyalty Classification, Nationality  
  - Top 5 Occupations by Loan Amount  
  - Loan segmentation by Banking Relationship and Advisor  

- **Deposit Analysis**
  - Deposit distribution by Income Band, Occupation, Loyalty Classification, Nationality  
  - Top 5 Occupations by Deposit Amount  
  - Deposit segmentation by Banking Relationship and Advisor  

---

## 🔎 Key Insights
- **Risk Concentration**: A small subset of clients hold disproportionately high loan balances, increasing default risk.  
- **Profitability Drivers**: High‑fee clients contribute significantly to deposits but have lower loan exposure.  
- **Demographic Patterns**: Younger clients show higher loan‑to‑income ratios, while senior clients maintain stronger deposit balances.  
- **Advisor Performance**: Certain advisors manage portfolios with higher risk exposure, useful for monitoring and training.  

---

## 🚀 Business Impact
- Enables banks to **identify high‑risk segments** and adjust lending strategies.  
- Provides **profitability analysis** by fee structure and relationship type.  
- Supports **data‑driven decision making** for client segmentation, advisor monitoring, and portfolio management.
